Hi, this is the personal website of Nicholas Rees.
I am an undergraduate studying math at UBC, expected to graduate Spring 2025. I am currently
looking for admission in Fall 2025 to graduate programs in pure mathematics. My interests are
largely related to analysis. Some current topics that I could see myself working on include problems in the intersection of harmonic analysis and geometric measure theory, such as those related to the Kakeya conjecture, Fourier restriction, and fractal geometry. I am also interested in related discrete
applications, such as in additive combinatorics, analytic number theory, and incidence geometry.

perspective). Outside of academics, I enjoy hiking in nearby mountains, rock climbing and
